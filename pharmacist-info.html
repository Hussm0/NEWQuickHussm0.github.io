How It Works for Prescribers

  • **Onboard & Verify:** Complete our secure onboarding process, including GPhC verification and indemnity checks.
  • **Go Online:** Access our intuitive portal (currently powered by Notion for MVP) and set your availability.
  • **Receive Consultations:** Patient requests are routed to you. Review symptoms and medical history.
  • **Assess & Prescribe:** Conduct virtual consultations and issue private prescriptions via SignatureRx if clinically appropriate.
  • **Earn:** Receive your share of the consultation fee directly.
